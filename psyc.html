The Psychic Game

How to play the game:

  1. Try to guess what letter Cartman is thinking!
  2. Start by clicking on any random letter, from a-z. If you guess correctly you win!
  3. You have 9 guesses to see if your psychic or not.
  4. At the end of each round Cartman will tell you the letter that he was thinking of.
